Mision

The Flying Dress in Cartagena's mission is to provide you with excellent photographic service and personalized attention, with a trained and professional team that will be with you at all times, providing high-quality photographs in historical locations throughout Cartagena.

The Flying Dress team in Cartagena consists of photographers and models providing excellent service during the photo shoot, moving around selected locations such as old houses in the historic city center, Getsemani, and Cartagena beaches.
